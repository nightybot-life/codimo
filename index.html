About Breads

Bread is a nutritious meal made out of flour, water, yeast, and salt. Real bread got only few ingredients like flour, water and salt. As per today there are atleast 6,000 unique bread varieties. It is used as a daily meal in many parts of the world. Bread is a staple food in many cultures and is often served with meals or used to make sandwiches. Bread can be made in various forms, including loaves, rolls, and flatbreads. It can be baked, steamed, or fried, and can be flavored with herbs, spices, or other ingredients.

History of bread

Bread was originated long ago around 6000 BC in southern Mesopotamia, cradle of the Sumerian civilization, who may have passed on knowledge of the Egyptians from around 3000 BC. Egyptions added products like yeast and honey to the bread. The Egyptians were the first to use yeast in bread making, which allowed for the development of leavened bread. The process of making bread has evolved over time, with different cultures and regions developing their own unique recipes and techniques. Today, bread is made in many different ways, using a variety of ingredients and methods, and is enjoyed by people all over the world.
